The central government 2 billion yuan to support rural elect

In 2015 the central government to arrange 2 billion special funds to support the Midwest, rural electronic commerce development of old revolutionary base areas, in particular, the use of money the key to the construction of county, township and village levelslogisticsDistribution system.

The Treasury will preferential support central and western provinces and 200 counties of old revolutionary base areas develop rural electricity comprehensive demonstrations, each county $10 million, and will be for examination to achieve the requirements of the demonstration county in 2016 to support a year.

A special fund will be specification used in the following three aspects: one is establishing perfect county, township and village levelslogisticsDistribution system;2 it is used to support the county public service center and e-commerce e-commerce service stations at the village level construction transformation, for the purpose of developing the rural e-commerce of agricultural and rural characteristic product brand cultivation and quality guarantee system construction, as well as the standardization of agricultural products, grading packing, pretreating and distribution facilities construction;3 it is used to support rural electronic commerce training.Among them the focus of the first item for the special funds tilt direction.

In addition, Wu Xiangyun particularly stressed that the central government funds shall not be used for the construction of network platform, as well as purchase flow, if there are any violation, will be disqualified from the comprehensive demonstration county, recover the fiscal funds.The Treasury the also specifically asked to avoid redundant construction and waste of resources.
