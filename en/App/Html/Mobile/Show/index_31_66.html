　　To distinguish the aviation accident risk and flight delay risks

　　In fact, aviation insurance and flight delay risks are different insurance products.

　　Air delay risk is a kind of property insurance, is in addition to its own reason, on the insured by'll board fixed flights were canceled or delayed reaches a certain time for economic compensation;Aviation accident risk is a kind of personal insurance, is on the insured in the insurance period internal cause aircraft accident injury, cause death or disability of the economic compensation.Many insurance companies will delay risks with tie-in sale, boat idea but consumers still need to confirm carefully when buy purchased danger to plant, so as not to miss opportunities for compensation.
