UPS Europe express market 1 billion euros The global Courier

It is reported that the United States UPSCourierThe company plans to invest 1 billion euros in the European market, to expand its express business network.A analysis says, this suggests that the major express delivery giant race may further escalation in the European market, may also face the global Courier industry, new change.

According to the German economic weekly quoted UPS Germany marketing director frank said, UPS will vigorously develop the German and European business in other parts of the network, is expected in the next three to five years to the European business investment of 1 billion euros, is mainly used for additional parcel delivery point, expansionlogisticsSorting center.

UPS a move aimed at expanding market share in Europe, also conforms to the global e-commerce continued to expand, the global Courier industry constantly integration trend of the market.Shortly after, federal express (FedEx) announced that will be 8 euros per share, total about 4.4 billion euros for the Dutch express delivery company TNT, the deal is expected to completed in the middle of 2016.Bloomberg reported that acquisition of fedex TNT will help expand air transportation and ground transportation network in Europe, so as to expand the market of parcel delivery service.

As Europe's second-biggest Courier company, poor performance of TNT in recent years has been the global express delivery giant as a key target, news from by other express giant in explaining many times before.It is understood that in 2013, UPS had planned to buy TNT for about 5.2 billion euros, but at that time European regulators worry that it will reduce the number of integrated delivery market in Europe, and mergers and acquisitions after the merger of the company will be more than 30% of Europe express delivery market, thereby inhibiting competition and lead to price increases, so there is no approved the merger request.Frank said, UPS has missed opportunity after the incident.

Analysis points out, the pace of the follow UPS fedex announced plans to expand the market, is also being the latter exchange the latest m&a brings challenge and pressure.The personage inside course of study, according to the comments UPS fedex did two years ago does not implement acquisition, future fedex's share of the European market will be improved, Europe express industry will usher in DHL, fedex, UPS three points all the time.It is understood that if fedex of the acquisition of TNT deal won regulatory approval, will occupy Europe express business deals 17% market share, second with more than UPS Europe express delivery industry, and DHL is still the top with a 19% market share.

According to the Washington post reported that in recent years, fedex have the upper hand in the competition with the UPS, thanks to its clear positioning and strong execution.Fedex, for example, last year bought the Supaswift company business in Africa and the reverselogisticsGenco, developing the African market and B2B logistics from the traditional to the electronic commerce field.The morning star international m analyst Keith's, a rating agency, says the two express giant takeover emphasis in the future may be on other business, such as contract logistics, freight forwarders, etc.

Europe more than 11 million people in the logistics industry, almost a quarter of the work and delivery in the post office branches.Europe's largest highway traffic volume, traffic volume of more than 46 million tons every day.Daily exports tobacco and drink more than 79 million euros.
