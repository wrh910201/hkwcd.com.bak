HK Import Express Services

 Our company as a Hongkong local company, can have the ability to  help you service of the Hong Kong to the mainland. Hong Kong  imports has two ways: general trade import declaration, another  is the way to express the import, each imports way has its  advantages.

    General trade customs declaration has the advantages of can  be large quantities of imported materials, and can be imposed by  the customs of all taxes and expenses relating to this paper. The related tax bill can be used to tax and export tax rebates

    Express the tax package imports its advantages are obvious. First of all, it does not require the shipper to provide the goods special documents. It is only required to provide the goods of ordinary commercial invoice and packing list so express import the required time and speed is faster, from receiving to import customs clearance of goods to the mainland generally in two to three working days.
