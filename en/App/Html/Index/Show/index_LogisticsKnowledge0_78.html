Push the inter-city logistics Internet +...

Their type：Logistics Knowledge 丨 Published:2015-06-30 丨 Reads:

In may this year, the ministry of commerce issued the "Internet + flow" plan of action.The relevant person in charge of the ministry of commerce said "action plan" will try to solve the two bottleneck problems.One is electrical contractor "the last kilometer" problem, such as the positive development of small and medium-sized cities and rural electricity, improve itCourierDistribution,logisticsWarehousing and other infrastructure, at the same time, encourage electricity enterprises to go out, through the construction of overseas warehouse, make overseaslogisticsSystem;The second is to break electricity "last one hundred meters" bottleneck.

Vehicle utilization models is not rational

Transportation department according to the latest data, the current logistics industry in China has more than 700 small micro logistics companies and more than 2000 freight drivers.Learned, was asymmetrical information between them, more than 600 one hundred million yuan each year in our spending and the effective utilization rate of vehicles is less than 50%, and the return empty.Change logistics car become to cut costs, one of the ways to improve utilization, replaced the traditional logistics car trucks for changan farce line such as light, not only can promote effective utilization, also can reduce costs, such as changan line 8 l / 100 km of ultra-low fuel consumption will be one 5 logistics companies to cut costs.

Business inventories in advance will be important distribution "the last kilometer"

Inventory lead businesses, including the category, scale and region, with the cheapest way to transport in advance to the consumer, after waiting for consumer place an order, from the latest electricity storage and complete the last mile distribution, in raising the efficiency of logistics at the same time, the use of light passenger car as inter-city logistics such as changan farce line, with its itself has not common loading space, low fuel consumption and other advantages to reduce logistics cost.

Rural electricity business has developed rapidly, to provide light passenger car such as logistics market

With the continuous development of electricity, three or four line city electric dealer market growing, which will expand the scope of logistics coverage.E-commerce narrowed the gap between urban and rural areas, rural users and the big city residents have the same shopping channels and logistics services.And restricted by the rural road width, the vehicle is highly restricted in some urban and rural, trucks and other logistics car very difficult to meet the rural logistics market, changan farce line such as light is happened to meet the urban and rural transport conditions and will occupy the important position in the logistics market in the countryside.

Action plan for "Internet + circulation" established including: support the development of the agricultural products brand construction and rural electricity services, encourage electricity enterprise integration convenient service facilities to carry out community existing electric business supporting services, encourage small and medium-sized city localization network service platform and service network construction important tasks, such as the implementation of the policy, to changan farce lines such as light guest provide opportunities and challenges in the development of logistics industry.
